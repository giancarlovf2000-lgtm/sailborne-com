2. Booking & Payment

2.1 Booking Process

All bookings begin with an inquiry submitted via our website, email, or phone. A booking is considered confirmed only upon receipt of the required deposit and written confirmation from SailBorne.

Availability is not guaranteed until a deposit is received. SailBorne reserves the right to cancel an unconfirmed booking and offer the date to another party.

2.2 Deposit Requirements

  • A deposit of 50% of the total charter fee is required to confirm your booking
  • The deposit must be received within 48 hours of booking confirmation
  • Failure to submit the deposit within this window may result in loss of the reserved date

2.3 Balance Payment

  • The remaining balance is due no later than 7 days before the charter date
  • For bookings made within 7 days of the charter, full payment is required immediately
  • Failure to pay the balance by the due date may result in cancellation without refund of the deposit

2.4 Accepted Payment Methods

  • Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Bank wire transfer (details provided upon booking confirmation)
  • Select digital payment platforms (inquire for current options)

2.5 Pricing

All prices are quoted in US Dollars (USD) and are subject to change without notice until a deposit is received. Quoted prices do not include gratuity (customarily 15–20% of charter fee), fuel surcharges (if applicable), port fees, or add-on services unless explicitly stated.

3.1 How to Cancel

All cancellations must be submitted in writing via email to kristen@sailborne.co. Verbal cancellations will not be accepted. The cancellation date is the date and time the written notice is received by SailBorne.

3.2 Rescheduling

Rescheduling requests made 7 or more days in advance may be accommodated without penalty, subject to availability. Rescheduling within 7 days of the charter date is treated as a cancellation and the above policy applies.

3.3 SailBorne-Initiated Cancellations

In the rare event that SailBorne must cancel your charter (mechanical failure, crew emergency, etc.), you will receive a full refund or a complimentary rescheduling at no additional cost. SailBorne is not liable for any consequential costs such as flights or accommodation.

4. Charter Rules & Conduct

4.1 Guest Responsibilities

  • All guests must be sober and in a physical condition suitable for water-based activities upon boarding
  • The client (booking party) is responsible for the conduct of all guests in their party
  • Any damage to the vessel or equipment caused by guests will be billed to the client at market repair rates
  • SailBorne reserves the right to refuse boarding or disembark any guest whose behavior poses a risk to safety

4.2 Alcohol & Substances

  • Guests may bring their own alcohol aboard for personal consumption
  • Excessive intoxication that compromises safety will not be tolerated
  • Illegal substances are strictly prohibited — violations will result in immediate termination of the charter with no refund

4.3 Capacity & Safety

  • Maximum guest capacity as specified at booking must not be exceeded
  • Children under 12 must wear a life jacket at all times on deck
  • Guests must follow all safety instructions from the captain and crew
  • The captain's decisions regarding safety and navigation are final

4.4 Prohibited Items

  • Illegal substances of any kind
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Single-use plastics beyond what is necessary (we are committed to ocean conservation)
  • Pets (unless pre-approved in writing)

6. Weather Policy

The safety of our guests and crew is our top priority. The captain has sole authority to make decisions regarding weather-related cancellations or route changes.

6.1 Weather Cancellations

  • If the captain determines conditions are unsafe, the charter may be cancelled or postponed
  • In the event of a weather cancellation, guests will receive a full rescheduling option with no fees
  • If rescheduling is not possible, a full refund will be issued

6.2 Route Modifications

The captain reserves the right to modify the planned route at any time due to weather, sea conditions, or safety concerns. Such modifications do not constitute grounds for a refund.

6.3 Guest-Initiated Weather Concerns

If a guest wishes to cancel due to forecast weather that the captain has not deemed unsafe, the standard cancellation policy applies. We encourage guests to trust our experienced captains' judgment regarding safe sailing conditions.
