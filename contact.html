Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your SailBorne experience.

We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance, especially for peak season (December–April in the Caribbean) and for larger groups or special events. That said, last-minute bookings may be available — contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
A 50% deposit is required to secure your charter date. The remaining balance is due 7 days before your departure. We accept major credit cards, bank transfer, and select digital payment methods.
Cancellations made 7 or more days before the charter receive a full refund. Cancellations 3–6 days prior receive a 50% refund. Cancellations under 48 hours are non-refundable. In the event of unsafe weather or conditions, the captain may cancel and we will work to fully reschedule your charter.
All charters include a licensed captain, dedicated crew, safety equipment, snorkeling gear, a premium sound system, complimentary beverages, and a personalized itinerary. Add-ons such as a private chef, jet skis, photography, or event décor are available at additional cost.
Absolutely — customization is what we do best. Tell us your preferred stops, activities, dining preferences, and any special occasions. Our team will build a personalized itinerary just for you, subject to weather and local conditions.
Complimentary soft drinks and water are included in all charters. Guests are welcome to bring their own food and alcohol aboard. For full catering with a gourmet dining experience, add our Private Chef service. Full-day and overnight charters include expanded dining options.
