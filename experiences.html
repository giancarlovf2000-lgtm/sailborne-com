Half-Day Charter

A perfect taste of luxury — 4 to 5 hours on the water, ideal for sunset cruises, small celebrations, or a spontaneous escape.

  • 4–5 hours on the water
  • Up to 16 guests
  • Snorkeling stops available
  • Light refreshments included
  • Flexible departure times

Full-Day Charter

An unforgettable full day at sea — 7 to 8 hours exploring coves, beaches, and open waters with everything on board.

  • 7–8 hours on the water
  • Multiple destination stops
  • Lunch & beverages included
  • Water sports equipment
  • Professional crew of 2–3
